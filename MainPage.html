SF MONOPOLY

Instructions

Basics

The object of the game is to become the wealthiest player through buying, renting, and selling of property. Each player begins the game with $1000. When one player runs out of money, the game ends, and the other player wins. High scores are awarded for particularly rich players who win the game on bankruptcy of their opponent.

Game Play
  1. Each player clicks the die to roll for first turn. The player with the highest number goes first. Each player starts at the corner marked "GO."
  2. The first player rolls the dice and his/her token moves the number of spaces indicated by the dice.
  3. The tokens remain on the spaces occupied and proceed from that point on the player's next turn. Two or more tokens may rest on the same space at the same time.
  4. Depending on the space your token reaches, you may be entitled to buy real estate, or be obliged to pay rent, draw a Chance or Community Chest card, Go To Jail, etc. according to the space.
Property and Rent
  1. Whenever you land on an unowned property, you may buy that property from the Bank at its printed price. If you decide to buy the property, the other player pays rent to you every time they land on that space, based on how much the land was worth when you bought it. Rent is one half of the property price if the property is owned by a player.
  2. If you choose not to buy the property that you have landed on, the other player gets a chance to buy the property.
  3. If neither you nor the other player buy the property once it has been landed on, then the bank takes control of the property and all players who land on that space after that turn pay rent to the bank at one quarter the property's original price.
Special Squares
  1. GOEach time a player's token lands on or passes over GO, whether by throwing the dice or drawing a card, the player collects $200.
  2. Free ParkingA player landing on this place does not receive any money, property or reward of any kind. This is just a "free" resting-place.
  3. Go to JailYou land in Jail when your token lands on the space marked "Go to Jail", or you draw a card marked "Go to jail." When you are sent to Jail you do not pass GO and do not collect $200. Your token moves directly to Jail and then your turn ends. To get out of jail for free, roll doubles on your next turn. Otherwise, pay a $50 fine.
  4. "Just Visiting" JailIf you happen to land on the Jail space during the normal course of your turn, you do not pay the fine or have to roll doubles to leave on your next roll. You are simply "visiting jail." This space otherwise does not receive any money, property or reward of any kind.
  5. Chance and Community ChestIf you land on a Chance or Community Chest space, you receive a card with random value associated with "Chance" or "Community Chest." Your turn ends after the instructions on the card are completed.
